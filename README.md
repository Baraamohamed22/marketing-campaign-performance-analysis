# Marketing Campaign Performance analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes a real-world marketing campaign dataset to understand customer behavior, revenue performance, campaign response, and product category performance. The goal is to transform raw marketing data into clear business insights and present them through an interactive performance marketing analysis-dashboard.

The dashboard helps answer key business questions such as:

- Which customer segments generate the most revenue?
- Which product categories perform best?
- How effective are marketing campaigns?
- How does income relate to customer spending?
- Which customer groups should be targeted for future campaigns?

## Tools and Technologies

- Python
- Pandas

## Dataset

The dataset contains customer demographic information, product spending, purchase behavior, campaign responses, and customer signup dates.

Main features include:

- Customer income
- Education level
- Marital status
- Product spending by category
- Number of purchases by channel
- Campaign acceptance history
- Latest campaign response
- Customer signup date

## Project Workflow

1. Loaded and explored the raw marketing campaign dataset.
2. Cleaned missing values and standardized column names.
3. Created new business metrics such as total spending, total purchases, income segments, and campaign response metrics.
4. Performed exploratory data analysis to identify revenue patterns and customer behavior.
5. Built an interactive HTML dashboard to visualize the most important marketing performance metrics.

## Dashboard Features

The dashboard includes:

- KPI cards for total revenue, customer count, average customer value, campaign response rate, and income-spending correlation.
- Filters for income segment, education, marital status, campaign response, and signup year.
- Revenue by income segment chart.
- Product revenue mix chart.
- Monthly revenue trend.
- Purchase channel performance.
- Income vs spending analysis.
- Campaign acceptance performance.
- Segment scorecard with revenue, average spend, customer count, and response rate.
- Strategic marketing recommendations.

## Key Insights

- Total revenue reached more than $1.35M.
- The High Income segment generated the highest revenue.
- Wines were the strongest product category, contributing about half of total product revenue.
- Income showed a strong positive relationship with customer spending.
- Campaign response can be improved by targeting high-value customer segments more effectively.

## Business Recommendations

- Focus marketing resources on high-income customers.
- Use Wines as a lead product category for premium campaigns.
- Cross-sell lower-performing product categories to high-value wine buyers.
- Improve campaign targeting using income, spending, and recency behavior.
- Build personalized campaigns for customers with strong purchase history.
